Robinson Helicopter Company is the world's leading manufacturer of civil helicopters and has produced over 4,000 helicopters operating in more than 70 countries.

Founded in 1973, Robinson currently produces two models of helicopter, the four-seat R44, the world's top-selling helicopter, and the two-seat R22, which has the lowest acquisition and operating costs of any production helicopter on the market today. Robinson owners and operators are supported by a network of more than 250 approved Robinson Dealers and Service Centers worldwide.
