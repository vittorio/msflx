Multiplayer

Whether you're interested in pylon racing, formation flying, coordinated search and rescue, live flight instruction, and live air traffic control, Multiplayer puts you right in a world full of other virtual pilots.

Multiplayer Tower (Deluxe only) lets you direct other Flight Simulator pilots or be directed by them over the Internet or a LAN.

Multiplayer Shared Aircraft lets you share a cockpit with another simulator pilot over the Internet or a LAN.
