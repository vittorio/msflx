Use the Instant Replay to see a portion of flight that you just flew. You can replay the flight at a speed of your choosing.

To open the Instant Replay dialog box

  • On the Options menu, click Instant Replay.


Replay that perfect flight segment.

To set the replay length

  1. Highlight the numbers in the Replay length box
  2. Type the number of seconds you want the replay to last.

- or -

  • Click the spin box next to the seconds box.

To set the replay speed

  1. Highlight the numbers in the Replay speed box.
  2. Type a number between 20 and 255.

- or -

  • Click the spin box next to the percent of actual speed box.

To see the replay repeat automatically

  • Select the Repeat replay continuously check box.
