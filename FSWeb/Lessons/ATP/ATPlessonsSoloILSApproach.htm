Solo Flight: Full ILS Approach
Fly This Lesson Now

Track inbound to the RITTS Outer Marker and then fly the full ILS approach to Runway 16R at Paine Field. Maintain 3000 until established on the approach. You are cleared to land on runway 16R.

You'll be flying in the Boeing 737.
